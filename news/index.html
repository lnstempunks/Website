Social Media

Our team's social media pages are updated regularly from January to March and once a week the rest of the year. Following us on these networks is the best way to keep up with our team.

On our pages, you can expect to see fun pictures of our students and mentors working on our latest projects, or our team visiting local schools and libraries. Our team's use of social media was recognized internationally by Comcast NBCUniversal's Media and Technology Innovation Award at the 2016 FIRST Championships.
