Smoky Mountains Regional Business Summit

What is the SMRBS?

The Smoky Mountains Regional Business Summit is an annual conference held in Knoxville, Tennessee that helps FIRST Robotics Competition teams grow their skills in the areas of finance, public relations, branding, team organization, and more. The event is hosted by team 3966, the L&N STEMpunks.

The conference aims to build skills in these often overlooked but critical aspects of having a successful FRC program. It will be a full day of learning, brainstorming, and networking with other teams in the region!

*Features special guest speakers LJ Robinson, Sydney Closser, and Sam McClanahan

Why should you come?

Veteran FRC students can have a peek into how other teams work, while new, inexperienced students can quickly gain these skills and propel your team to success. The Business Summit is a great opportunity for all of your students, no matter what they do on your team.

The video to the right showcases last year's Business Summit.

Where, when, and how much is it?

The 2015 Smoky Mountains Regional Business Summit will be held on September 19th from 8:30am to 4:00pm at the Sarah Simpson Professional Development/Technology Center in South Knoxville, Tennessee at 801 Tipton Ave. 37920 (shown above).

The fee is $175 per team to cover 5 members. Teams may bring up to 10 members with a fee of $10 per extra member (ex: 7 members would cost $195). The fee covers breakfast, lunch, and a t-shirt for each attendee.
