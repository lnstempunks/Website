Why Sponsor Us?

Our team relies on generous donations from local businesses to compete each year in the FIRST Robotics Competition. Our operating costs include competition fees, buying shirts and costumes, transportation, and much more. By sponsoring our team, you are enabling the next generation of engineers and scientists.

Sponsors on our 2014 shirt.

How Does It Benefit You?

Depending on your sponsorship level, our team has different ways to show our appreciate for your generous sponsorship of our team. These options include: your company logo on our t-shirt, website and robot, personalized thank-you cards, 3D printed paperweights, and more. You can read more about sponsorship levels below.

Our team in the Veteran's Day Parade.

Sponsorship Levels

We offer four tiers of sponsorships; Bronze, Silver, Gold and Platinum. Each level is based on your donation amount, either monetary or based on the value of products donated. Each level offers different benefits for your company. Bronze sponsorship is a $100-$250 contribution, silver is a $250-$500 contribution, gold is a $500-$1500 contribution, and platinum is a $1500 or higher contribution.
